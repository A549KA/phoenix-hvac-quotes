Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

Desert Comfort Quotes is an independent quote-matching website for Phoenix-area HVAC services. We collect information you submit through our forms, such as your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, service need, timeline, and project details.

How we use information

  • To match your request with local HVAC companies.
  • To contact you about your quote request.
  • To improve our website and lead-matching process.

Sharing

When you submit a request and consent to contact, we may share your information with local HVAC providers so they can contact you about your project. We do not sell unrelated personal information for unrelated marketing.

TCPA consent

By submitting a form with the consent box checked, you authorize Desert Comfort Quotes and participating service providers to contact you by phone, text, or email about your request. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact us through the website form.
