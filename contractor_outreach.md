Pilot offer: I can send the first few qualified leads at no charge so you can judge quality. If they’re useful, we can do exclusive pay-per-lead or appointment-based pricing.

Would you be open to a quick 10-minute call this week?

Best,
Desert Comfort Quotes

## Call opener
Hi, this is about sending qualified Phoenix homeowners looking for AC repair or replacement quotes. Are you the right person to discuss new customer leads?

## Qualification questions for buyer
1. Which areas do you want? Phoenix, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Scottsdale, etc.
2. Which jobs do you prefer? Repair, replacement, heat pump, maintenance?
3. Minimum lead requirements? homeowner only, system age, financing, etc.
4. How fast can you respond to emergency leads?
5. Preferred lead delivery: email, SMS, phone call, CRM webhook?
6. Pricing preference: per lead, per booked appointment, monthly territory?

## Pricing proposal
- Free pilot: first 3-5 qualified leads.
- Exclusive repair lead: $75-$125.
- Exclusive replacement/heat pump lead: $150-$300.
- Booked appointment: $250-$500.
- Territory rental after proof: $1,500-$5,000/month.
