When to Replace Your AC

Signs you need AC replacement: system over 12-15 years old, frequent repairs, rising energy bills, uneven cooling, or use of R-22 refrigerant (which is being phased out).

Cost of AC Replacement in Phoenix

Phoenix AC replacement costs typically range from $4,000-$15,000. Factors include system size, efficiency rating, brand, and installation complexity. Get multiple quotes to find the best value.

Energy-Efficient AC Options

Modern AC systems offer SEER2 ratings of 15-26+. High-efficiency models can reduce cooling costs by 20-40% compared to older systems. SRP and APS offer rebates for qualifying models.

Frequently Asked Questions About Phoenix HVAC

Do I need a permit for AC replacement in Phoenix?

Yes. The City of Phoenix and Maricopa County require permits for AC replacement. Your contractor should include permit fees in their quote and handle all paperwork.

How long does AC replacement take?

AC replacement typically takes 1-2 days. Same-day replacement is sometimes possible for straightforward installations.
