# Phoenix HVAC Lead-Gen MVP Plan

## Karar
**Niş:** Phoenix, Arizona — Emergency AC Repair + HVAC Replacement + Heat Pump Upgrade leads.

## Neden bu niş?
- Phoenix yaz sıcaklığı yüksek; AC arızası acil ve yüksek niyetli aramadır.
- HVAC replacement/heat pump install yüksek biletli iştir ($7k-$15k+ tipik proje).
- Yerel firmalar hızlı lead almak ister; lead başı tahmini değer:
  - AC repair: $50-$150
  - Replacement/heat pump: $150-$400+
- Formla iyi qualify edilir: zip, homeowner, sistem yaşı, aciliyet, problem tipi, telefon/SMS izni.

## Marka / Site
**Brand:** Desert Comfort Quotes
**Domain önerileri:**
- desertcomfortquotes.com
- phoenixacquotes.com
- azcoolingquotes.com

## Offer
"Compare 2-3 local Phoenix AC quotes in minutes. Emergency repair, replacement, and heat pump upgrades."

## Funnel
1. Landing page: Phoenix AC/heat pump problem + trust + form.
2. Multi-step form:
   - Zip code
   - Need type: repair / replacement / heat pump / maintenance
   - Urgency: today / 1-3 days / this week / planning
   - Homeowner? yes/no
   - System age
   - Name, phone, email
   - TCPA consent checkbox
3. Lead storage:
   - MVP: static demo form saves to browser/localStorage and can be wired to Formspree/Tally/Airtable/Zapier.
   - Production: webhook → Airtable/Google Sheets/CRM + call tracking.
4. Seller outreach:
   - Start with 15 Phoenix HVAC companies.
   - Offer 5 free test leads or pay-per-closed-appointment pilot.

## Monetization
Pilot pricing:
- Shared repair lead: $35-$75
- Exclusive repair lead: $75-$125
- Replacement/heat pump lead: $150-$300
- Appointment-set lead: $250-$500
- Monthly territory rental after proof: $1,500-$5,000/mo per category/suburb cluster.

Negative keywords:

## Compliance notes
- Add TCPA consent for calls/SMS.
- Do not impersonate contractors.
- Clearly state independent quote-matching service.
- Privacy policy and terms needed before paid launch.
- For Google Business Profile, must have legitimate local/service-area business representation; avoid fake address.

## 7-day execution plan
Day 1: Launch landing page + form + privacy/terms placeholders.
Day 2: Create Airtable/Sheet CRM + webhook.
Day 3: Build 10 suburb SEO pages from template.
Day 4: Contact 15-30 contractors; secure 1-3 pilot buyers.
Day 5: Add call tracking number and lead routing rules.
Day 6: Start small Google Ads exact/phrase campaign.
Day 7: Review CPL, lead quality, buyer feedback; adjust form and keywords.
